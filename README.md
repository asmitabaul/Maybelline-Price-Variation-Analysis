# Maybelline Price Variation Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes pricing and discount strategies for Maybelline products across multiple Indian e-commerce platforms, including Amazon, Flipkart, Nykaa, Myntra, Purplle, and the official Maybelline website.

The objective was to identify price variations, evaluate discounting strategies, and understand how platform-specific pricing impacts consumer purchasing decisions.

---

## Objectives

* Compare product pricing across major e-commerce platforms
* Analyze discount strategies used by different platforms
* Evaluate price differences for identical products
* Study the relationship between discounts and final selling prices

---

## Dataset

The dataset was manually collected and compiled using Microsoft Excel.

### Product Categories

* Foundation
* Lipstick
* Mascara

### Platforms

* Amazon
* Flipkart
* Nykaa
* Myntra
* Purplle
* Maybelline Official Website

---

## Methodology

1. Collected pricing data across multiple platforms.
2. Standardized product information and discount values.
3. Calculated average MRP, discount percentage, and final selling price.
4. Built interactive Power BI dashboards for platform-level and product-level analysis.
5. Compared discount strategies and pricing behavior across platforms.

---

## Key Findings

* Significant price variations exist for identical products across platforms.
* Platforms such as Amazon and Flipkart generally offered higher discounts and lower final prices.
* Nykaa followed a comparatively premium pricing strategy.
* A clear inverse relationship was observed between discount percentage and final selling price.
* Consumers can achieve substantial savings through cross-platform comparison before purchase.
